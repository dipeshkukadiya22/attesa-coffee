Bridging Farmers and Roasters

Fostering transparent partnerships and delivering quality from origin to roastery — our mission is to connect coffee communities through trust and traceability.

Our Story

Attesa Coffee is a green coffee importer based in the Netherlands, specializing in responsibly sourced and fully traceable specialty coffee. Our team, spread across Europe, South America, and Asia, brings deep expertise and cultural diversity to our operations.

We work directly with reliable farmers and producers at origin, building long-term, transparent relationships. Our coffees are evaluated both at origin and in the Netherlands to ensure consistent quality.

Our Mission

To reduce the distance between producers and roasters by building transparent, responsible, and resilient supply chains.

Our Vision

A global coffee community rooted in care, trust, and traceability — connecting farmers, roasters, and coffee lovers around the world.

Our Values

Transparency. Fairness. Ethical sourcing. Customer-first thinking. And always shared learning and long-term partnerships.
