About Us

Life at Attesa

Get to know who we are, how we work, and why our people thrive — from culture to coffee perks.

who we are

Born in Amsterdam, Attesa Coffee bridges the gap between origin and roaster with integrity and purpose. We work closely with producers in over 14 countries to deliver responsibly sourced, high-quality green coffee — with a strong focus on traceability and transparency wherever possible.

Working at Attesa means being part of a focused team with a big mission: to make green coffee better for people, for partners, and for the planet.

We’re coffee people, but also data minds, logistics thinkers, and relationship builders. Our culture is founded on trust, ownership, and a shared commitment to doing things right.

Team Benefits

  • Opportunities with international exposure
  • Coffee education
  • Daily specialty brews at the office
  • Transparent, people-first workplace
  • Supportive, tight-knit team environment

Our Culture

At Attesa, we champion curiosity, ownership, and shared growth.

We’re a collaborative team that values ideas, embraces diverse perspectives, and builds relationships rooted in trust, with each other and with the coffee community.

Whether you're interested in sourcing, business development, or strategy, you'll find space to grow, contribute, and belong — all while staying close to the coffee.

Doors that are Open

Openings at our offices you can grab one from.

Business Development Intern

Amsterdam, The Netherlands

As a Business Development Intern in Amsterdam, you’ll assist in market research, outreach, and supporting new client opportunities. This role offers hands-on experience in coffee trade and global partnerships—perfect for proactive learners.

Quality Control Intern

Amsterdam, The Netherlands

Based in Amsterdam, you’ll assist the QC team in sampling, evaluating, and documenting green and roasted coffees. You’ll gain practical insight into sensory analysis and quality assurance across diverse origins and roasters.

Office Executive Assistant

Amsterdam, The Netherlands

Join the Amsterdam office to support scheduling, documentation, and day-to-day coordination across teams. You’ll learn the internal workings of a lean, global coffee business while helping operations run smoothly.

Sales Executive Intern

Europe

In this Europe-based role, you’ll drive new leads, strengthen client relationships, and support strategic sales initiatives. This is a great opportunity to build industry knowledge and contribute directly to business growth.
