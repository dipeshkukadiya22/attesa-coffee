At Attesa Coffee, every cup tells a story — and it starts long before the roast.

Posted by Maddi Britton

At Attesa Coffee, we don’t just serve coffee — we serve stories. Behind every cup lies a journey across continents, through communities, and into your hands with care.

We began our journey in Amsterdam with a simple question: What would coffee look like if we put relationships at the center? 

That question led us far — to the red, sun-kissed soils of East Africa, the misty mountains of Central America, and the volcanic slopes of Indonesia. What we found wasn’t just coffee. It was a deep, resilient network of farmers, families, and traditions — each bean holding a legacy waiting to be told.

From Origin to Amsterdam — A Transparent Journey

We work directly with producers in over 10 countries. These aren’t just suppliers; they’re partners. Every farm we source from practices responsible, sustainable growing, often passed down through generations. From selective hand-picking to drying beds and fermentation techniques, we witness these methods firsthand — ensuring the coffee is not only delicious but also fairly and ethically sourced.

We believe traceability is non-negotiable. You should know where your coffee comes from, who grew it, and how. That’s why each Attesa lot comes with full transparency — from farm location to processing method, even tasting notes and harvest year.

Roasted for Character, Not Uniformity

Our roastery in Amsterdam isn’t a production line — it’s a creative studio. Every bean is profiled and cupped meticulously. We don’t roast to mask flaws. We roast to amplify origin.  That means letting the blueberry pop in a natural Yirgacheffe, preserving the crisp citrus of a washed Guatemalan, or coaxing out that rich hazelnut in a Brazilian lot.

We roast small batches, often experimenting with roast curves, airflows, and charge temps to bring out complexity without compromise. There’s no one-size-fits-all here — just a commitment to craftsmanship.

Brewing Community, Not Just Coffee

Coffee isn’t just a product. It’s a shared experience. Whether it’s sipped in a quiet moment, shared with a friend, or brewed behind a bar — it connects people.

That’s why we invest in more than beans. We invest in people.

We host regular cuppings and training sessions in our roastery, and offer origin trip opportunities to baristas and wholesale partners. Because we believe that the more we all understand coffee, the more meaning we can pour into every cup.

And for our customers — from the home brewer to the third-wave café — we’re here to guide, educate, and inspire. Through workshops, tasting guides, and this blog, we’re building an open, curious coffee culture rooted in transparency and trust.
